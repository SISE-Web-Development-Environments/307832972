About Me

SABINA HOFMAN

I was born in Poland in 1935, during the Holocaust I hid in various villages with Polish families, lost my father and mother and the fate of my brother is unknown to this day. After the war, at the age of 10, I was taken to a Jewish orphanage near the town of Lodge where I met my best friend Esti that I am in contact with to this day. In 1948, I immigrated to Israel by the Jewish organization "Coordinating". At first I stayed at the 'Oninin' institution near Kfar Saba and then came to Ben Shemen Youth Village. I served in the 'Nahal' forces in Yad Mordechai kibbutz. In 1953, I arrived to Kibbutz Shoval, got to know Avri, me got married and we live together at Kibbutz to this day. Over the years at the kibbutz, I worked as the kibbutz secretary, responsible for the commune, and the nursery. I have a great family, 4 children (Ronen, Tsafrir, Marganit and Nimrod) and 10 grandchildren. Today I am a happy person, I am busy with family and I am happy. I love to knit, read books, do lots of sports and have fun with my family and husband.
